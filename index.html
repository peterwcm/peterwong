About
I am a Full Stack Web Developer who happens to like design.

Why does it matter? When it comes to web design, it's not just about making the website "pretty", it's more about usability and user experience. Is the text large enough for people to read? Can users navigate the site easily without any instruction? Basically, you don't want to have poorly designed website that could potentially confuse users and hence, affect the conversion rate and decrease your sales.

Web Development

I have more than 5 years of experience in web development. From API to front-end development. I have not only worked in big enterprise companies like Flight Centre, but also started my own businesses including Zippity and Blossom Days.
